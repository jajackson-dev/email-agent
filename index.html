What OpsThreads does

Built for the way small
businesses actually run.

No consultants. No 200-page manuals. Just clear, living SOPs your whole team can follow.

AI organizes your SOPs
Paste in your existing docs, voice notes, or rough instructions — OpsThreads structures them into clear, step-by-step procedures automatically.
Always up to date
SOPs drift out of date fast. OpsThreads flags outdated steps and suggests revisions based on how your team actually works.
Your whole team on the same page
Give every employee one place to find the right process — whether they're in the office, remote, or brand new to the role.
Ask questions, get answers
Employees can ask "how do I process a refund?" and get the exact SOP steps — no digging through folders or pinging managers.
Organized by department
Group SOPs by team, process type, or priority — so Operations, HR, and Finance each have their own clean section.
Version history built in
Every change is tracked. Roll back to a previous version any time, or see exactly who changed what and when.
